CASH FEVER

Let the bags of gold and stacks of cash in Cash Fever bring you riches! Match numbers to win prizes. Golden Stars bring instant wins and maybe even double the win! This game is packed with two sparkling bonus rounds full of glittering prizes: strike it rich in the Wheel Bonus, where your prize is multiplied up to 10x, or reveal and collect cash prizes beneath the Gold Bars in the Prize Bonus.

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Match any Your Number to any Lucky Number and win the prize amount shown below.

Reveal a Gold Star Instant Win symbol to instantly win the prize amount shown below.

Reveal a Gold Star 2x Multiplier symbol to double the prize amount shown below.

Find a Golden Wheel symbol to trigger the Wheel Bonus.

Find a Gold Bars symbol to trigger the Prize Bonus.

In the Wheel Bonus, spin the wheel to reveal a prize and a corresponding multiplier of up to 10x

In the Prize Bonus, reveal the prizes hidden within the Gold Bars. Revealing the coin stack ends the game and collects the total of the revealed prize amounts.

The game ends when all Lucky Numbers, Your Numbers, and any triggered bonus items are revealed. A message appears, indicating the game's outcome.

The game ends when all Lucky Numbers, Your Numbers, and bonus items are revealed.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
